Our Story

From Our Family to Yours

The Beginning

From our family and friends along the North American coast, bonding over a shared love for highland tequila, we come together to celebrate this fine agave-forward spirit.

Bringing into existence an artisanal brand that puts flavor first. Coming from within the walls of a multi-generation distillery located in Arandas, Jalisco, we are proud to serve you our pure and earthy tequila that hits you with a subtle, but sneaky complexity.

"As all great spirits do."

From our family to yours, enjoy!

Two Partners, One Vision

FILAZUL was founded by two partners united by a passion for exceptional tequila and a deep appreciation for the craft that goes into every bottle.

One of our founders carries with him the heritage of Los Altos de Jalisco, with deep family roots in Tepatitlán. This connection runs through our veins—we are alteños, people of the highlands, and this identity shapes everything we do.

What began as gatherings among friends along the North American coast, sharing stories over glasses of highland tequila, evolved into a mission: to bring an authentic, flavor-first tequila to those who appreciate quality and craft.

[Image: Founders/Highland Landscape]

Alteño Heritage

To be an alteño is to carry the spirit of the highlands in your heart. Los Altos de Jalisco is more than just a region—it's a way of life, a tradition of excellence, and a commitment to craft that spans generations.

Our family roots in Tepatitlán connect us to the land, the people, and the centuries-old tradition of tequila making. This isn't just where we source our tequila—this is where we come from.

The highlands have shaped us, and in turn, we honor that heritage with every bottle of FILAZUL. We understand the terroir, we know the masters, and we respect the process because it's part of who we are.

"We are not just making tequila in the highlands—
we are alteños making tequila from our homeland."

Why Arandas

Arandas sits at the heart of Los Altos, renowned worldwide for producing some of Mexico's finest tequila. The elevation, climate, and limestone-rich soil create ideal conditions for growing exceptional blue agave.

Our partnership with a multi-generation distillery in Arandas allows us to work with master craftsmen who have dedicated their lives to perfecting their art. Their award-winning expertise, combined with our selective standards, ensures every bottle of FILAZUL meets our vision of what highland tequila should be.

Our Philosophy

At FILAZUL, everything begins and ends with flavor. We believe that exceptional tequila doesn't need to hide behind elaborate aging or complex infusions—the agave should speak for itself.

That's why we focus on an agave-forward expression that showcases the pure character of highland blue agave. Subtle, yet with sneaky complexity, our Blanco reveals layers of flavor that unfold with each sip.

Flavor First

Every decision we make—from agave selection to production methods—is guided by one question: will this make the tequila taste better? Quality over everything.

Selective Sourcing

Working with a distillery that has access to abundant, high-quality agave allows us to be exceptionally selective. We choose plants based on sugar content and flavor characteristics, ensuring consistency and excellence.

Highland Terroir

The limestone aquifers of Los Altos provide pristine water drawn from deep wells. Combined with ideal elevation and climate, our agave develops higher sugar content and natural complexity.

Artisanal Craft

Multi-generational expertise meets modern precision. Small-batch production ensures quality control, while award-winning master distillers bring decades of knowledge to every batch.
