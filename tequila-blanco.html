Pandan Suntan

A tropical twist on the classic Tequila Sunrise that celebrates FILAZUL's distinctive pandan notes. Bright, refreshing, and showcasing the sneaky complexity of our Blanco.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz FILAZUL Blanco
  • 4 oz fresh orange juice
  • 0.5 oz pandan syrup
  • 0.5 oz fresh lime juice
  • 0.25 oz grenadine
  • Orange slice and pandan leaf for garnish

Instructions

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice
  2. Add FILAZUL Blanco, orange juice, pandan syrup, and lime juice
  3. Stir gently to combine
  4. Slowly pour grenadine over the back of a spoon so it settles at the bottom
  5. Garnish with orange slice and fresh pandan leaf
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy the sunset gradient
